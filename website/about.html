About Shaitanoff Non-Profit

We’re a small, independent studio that believes the best websites are the simplest ones. Founded in 2018, we’ve helped startups, non-profits, and local businesses put their best foot forward online.

Our philosophy is straightforward: ship fast, keep it accessible, and never make a visitor wait. We build with the fundamentals of the web — clean HTML, thoughtful CSS, and just enough JavaScript — so the things we make stay fast and easy to maintain for years.
